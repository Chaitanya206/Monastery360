About Monastery360

Our Vision

Monastery360 aims to digitally preserve the rich cultural heritage of Sikkim’s monasteries, making them accessible to tourists, researchers, and spiritual seekers across the globe. We believe in combining technology with tradition to create an immersive experience.

Our Mission & Impact

Our platform provides 360° virtual tours, interactive maps, and digital archives of manuscripts and murals. By doing so, we help boost tourism sustainably, preserve endangered cultural artifacts, and educate the world about Sikkim’s spiritual and architectural treasures.

We also empower local communities by including them in content creation, storytelling, and digital archiving, ensuring that the heritage remains alive both physically and digitally.

Why Monastery360?

  • Immersive digital experiences for each monastery.
  • Offline access for remote locations.
  • Integration with local tourism and cultural events.
  • AI-powered search and categorization of historical content.
  • Supports education, research, and spiritual exploration worldwide.
